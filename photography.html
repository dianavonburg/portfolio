Photography

Visual narratives through composition and light — capturing moments that tell stories.

Visual Storytelling

Photography focused on narrative, composition, and color

My photography captures the rhythm of urban life and the serenity of natural landscapes. Each image is carefully composed to tell a story — whether it's the leading lines of San Francisco's cable cars cutting through Market Street or the gentle texture of waves at sunset in Pacifica.

I focus on finding beauty in everyday moments, using light and composition to create images that resonate emotionally with viewers. My work spans urban photography, seascapes, and documentary-style captures that highlight both the energy of city life and the tranquil moments found in nature.

My Approach

Composition First

Every shot begins with strong compositional elements — leading lines, rule of thirds, and natural framing to guide the viewer's eye.

Natural Light

I work primarily with available light, finding the magic in golden hour, blue hour, and the interplay of shadows and highlights.

Emotional Connection

Each image should evoke a feeling — whether it's the energy of urban life or the peaceful contemplation of natural scenes.

Technical Expertise

Equipment & Tools

I work with professional-grade equipment to ensure the highest quality captures. My workflow emphasizes both technical precision and creative vision.

Post-Processing Philosophy

My editing approach enhances natural beauty without over-processing. I use Adobe Lightroom and Photoshop to refine exposure, color grading, and composition while maintaining the authentic feel of each moment.

Specialties

  • Urban landscape and street photography
  • Seascape and natural environment captures
  • Golden hour and blue hour photography
  • Compositional storytelling through leading lines
  • Color harmony and tonal balance
  • Documentary-style environmental portraits
