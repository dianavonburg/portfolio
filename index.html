About Me

I’m Diana Von Burg — a creator, designer, and entrepreneur passionate about blending innovation, art, and storytelling. With a background in industrial engineering and years of hands-on experience in design, photography, and content creation, I’ve built projects that combine technical precision with creativity.

Gatillo Design. As the founder of Gatillo Design, I engineered and 3D-printed the Ultimate Adapter for circular knitting machines (Addi & Centro). This product converts manual machines into motorized tools — improving speed and finish. I craft installation manuals, line-art diagrams, and video tutorials that document the process from concept to product.

Content Creation. I run two YouTube channels: Knitting with Diana (tutorials, patterns, innovation) and Yoga con Diana (yoga, pranayama, and meditation). I’m also a visual storyteller — scripting, filming, and editing videos that feel authentic and visually striking.

Skills & Tools: Fusion 360, 3D printing, photography, videography, Photoshop, Lightroom, Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, Canva, FinalCut, Keynote. Bilingual: Spanish (native), Portuguese (fluent), English (fluent).
