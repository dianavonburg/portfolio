About Me

Blending innovation, art, and storytelling

I’m Diana Von Burg — a creator, designer, and entrepreneur with a background in industrial engineering. I combine technical precision with creativity across product design, photography, and visual storytelling.

Gatillo Design. I engineered and 3D‑printed the Ultimate Adapter for circular knitting machines (Addi & Centro), converting manual machines into motorized tools. I design installation manuals, line‑art diagrams, and demo videos end‑to‑end.

Content Creation. I run two channels — Knitting with Diana and Yoga con Diana — focused on tutorials, patterns, yoga, and meditation. I script, film, and edit polished videos that feel authentic and engaging.
